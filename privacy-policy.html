Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who we are

PDFplusTools provides free online tools for PDFs, images, invoices, text, calculators and everyday document tasks. In this policy, “PDFplusTools,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to the operator of this website.

For privacy or support questions, please use the Contact page.

How our tools work

Many PDFplusTools features are designed to run directly in your browser. For browser-based tools, selected files are processed locally by your browser and are not intentionally uploaded to our server.

Uploaded files and file processing

For browser-based tools, files are handled locally in your browser. We do not intentionally store those files. Avoid processing highly sensitive or confidential files unless you understand how the specific tool works and accept the risk.

Invoice and OCR tools

Invoice extraction and OCR tools can produce inaccurate results depending on image quality, layout, language, scan quality, rotation and formatting. Users must review and verify all extracted information before using it for accounting, tax, audit, payment or business purposes.

The website may use cookies, local storage or similar technologies for basic functionality and user experience. If analytics, advertising or payment services are added later, this policy will be updated to explain those services.

Payments and advertising

PDFplusTools is currently provided as a free website. We do not currently process payments or sell subscriptions through the website.

We may use third-party services for hosting, security, content delivery, browser libraries, deployment or performance. These services may process limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

Data retention

Files processed by browser-based tools are not intentionally retained by us. Contact messages may be retained for as long as reasonably needed to respond and maintain support records.

Security

We use reasonable steps to keep the website safe, including browser-based processing where possible and limiting data collection. No online service is completely secure.

Your privacy rights
